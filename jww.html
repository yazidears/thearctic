Here's an interactive, minimalistic website that generates random haikus based on a list of predefined words. This is a simple and playful concept that combines a unique interaction and fun characters (words) into a whimsical experience. Viewers can click on the "Generate Haiku" button to create a new blend of beauty and randomness in 5-7-5 syllables. For simplicity, the list of words is included within the script and their syllables are pre-calculated. ```html
Click the button below to generate a random haiku.
``` This short script will grab a random word from the list, and add it to the line if the syllables of the line + the syllables of the new word stay within or reach the limit. When all the lines have been generated according to the haiku syllable structure, the new haiku is displayed on the screen. Each click on the button will generate a new random haiku.
